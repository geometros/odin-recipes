Lasagna

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ricotta
  • Parmesan
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  1. Boil noodle
  2. In a skillet on medium high, brown ground beef. Season the beef
  3. Season the beef and deglaze with sauce
  4. Line a baking dish with cooked noodle
  5. Alternate layers of noodle, ricotta, beef sauce
  6. Top with parmesan
  7. Bake at 350 until heated thru
